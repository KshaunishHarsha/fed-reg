Federal Register Sentinel

Regulatory Monitoring for Animal Advocates

Every morning the Sentinel scans 7 federal agencies, filters for animal-relevant documents through a keyword + AI pipeline, and delivers a plain-language digest to the ALDF policy team.

1

Ingest

Pulls that day's Federal Register publications from USDA, EPA, FWS, and 4 other agencies via the official FR API.

2

Filter & Summarize

A 4-layer keyword + AI pipeline identifies animal-relevant documents and generates plain-language advocacy summaries.

3

Deliver

Compiles a structured digest — Action Required, Watchdog Monitoring, Potential Matches — and emails it to subscribers.
